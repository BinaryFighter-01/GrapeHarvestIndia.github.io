About GrapeHarvest India

We are a family-owned business dedicated to bringing the freshest, highest-quality grapes from the vineyards of Nashik, Maharashtra, directly to your table. Established in 2020, our mission is to celebrate India’s rich grape-growing heritage while supporting local farmers.

Our grapes are hand-picked and delivered with care, ensuring you enjoy the natural sweetness and flavor of every bunch. Whether it’s Thompson Seedless, Flame Seedless, or Bangalore Blue, we’ve got something for every grape lover!
